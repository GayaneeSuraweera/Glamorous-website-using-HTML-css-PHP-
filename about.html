About Us

          Founded by Mrs. Roshel Fernando(CEO), Glamorous.lk is an online led fashion retail store which brings you unique and elegant designs to add a touch of glamour to your everyday life!!!

Glamorous will be introducing a variety of new styles and collections each month, and each season keeping up with the ongoing trends and fashion demands while maintaining unique designs at all times.
